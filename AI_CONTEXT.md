## Current Product Rules
- There are 3 quinielas with independent winners:
- 1) Group stage
- 2) Round of 32 + Round of 16
- 3) Final phases (Quarterfinals, Semifinals, Third Place, Final)

## UI Direction
- Mobile compact prediction cards must prioritize legibility of team flags and names.
- Mobile score input should avoid overcrowded controls; direct numeric input is preferred.

## Knockout ID Mapping
- Knockout predictions are persisted as synthetic IDs (`match_1000`, `match_1001`, ...).
- Synthetic IDs depend on knockout matches ordered by `matchDate ASC`.
